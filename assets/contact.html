What is your development process like?
My process is collaborative and transparent. It typically involves an initial consultation, planning, design approval, development, rigorous testing, and finally, launch and ongoing support. I keep you informed at every stage.
How long does it typically take to build a website?
The timeline depends entirely on the project's scope and complexity. A simple brochure or portfolio website might take 1-2 days, while a more complex e-commerce or custom web application could take 1-4 weeks. We'll establish a clear timeline together after our initial discussion.
What technologies and tools do you specialize in?
I build modern, responsive websites using core web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and powerful frameworks like React. I also have experience with backend integration and Content Management Systems (CMS) like WordPress for client-friendly editing.
Do you only build the website, or do you handle hosting and maintenance too?

I offer end-to-end solutions. I can build your site and also provide guidance on securing reliable hosting. I offer ongoing maintenance and support plans to ensure your site stays updated, secure, and runs smoothly after launch.

How do we get started?
Getting started is easy! Simply reach out to me through the contact form or email. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project vision, goals, and requirements, and I can provide a detailed estimate.
